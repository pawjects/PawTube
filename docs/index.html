info About PawTube

PawTube is a clean, distraction-free YouTube front-end built for people who want to watch videos without algorithmic rabbit holes, intrusive ads, or cluttered interfaces. Just paste a link or search by name — it is that simple.

Privacy & Data

PawTube stores only your watch history locally in your browser. No accounts, no tracking, no data collection. Your viewing habits stay on your device. History can be cleared anytime from the History page.

Built with vanilla JavaScript and the Piped API. Not affiliated with YouTube.
